Privacy Policy

VTEE is a platform for various users to apply with their profiles to map available job opportunities provided by the Kerala State Scheduled Tribes Development Department. Our privacy policy applies to all members using or visiting our services.

Services This privacy policy applies to all the members who are using VTEE App.

Change Any changes done to the privacy policy of VTEE will apply to your use of services after the effective date. VTEE has the right to modify this privacy policy. For any reason if we make changes to it, we will let you know by sending a notice, or by any alternative ways, to help you provide the opportunity to review the changes before they become effective. If you can’t agree with our changes, then you can close your account.

DATA WE COLLECT The Data you provide us For creating an account in VTEE you need to provide some data to us which includes personal details, academic details, interests etc. and are being used to map possible job opportunities to the users in the coming versions of the application.

Registration To create an account in VTEE you need to provide some data like your name, and mobile number as well as answers to all the question fields provided in the application.

Profile Your profile information is something that adds value to your account. In VTEE while creating the profile, you will be asked with a set of questions which will be used for the scope of working of the application. What information does VTEE obtain and how is it used? VTEE may collect some information about your profile, like contact methods, device type, and information about the way you use the Application etc. This information may be used by third parties to customize new features in this app. The information also may be used for market or other research regarding aggregated traffic patterns.

Security The data is safely encrypted and stored with the Kerala State Scheduled Tribes Development Department and will be used only for the scope of this project. This won’t be shared to any third parties for any uses. The data will be solely handled by stakeholders of the project.

Messages When you send, receive, or engage with messages in connection with our services, we’ll collect some details about you. We’ll notify you with new openings, and important details via messages.

Other Our services are dynamic, since we frequently introduce new features to the app. If we gather personal data or change how we collect, use or share your data, we will notify you and may also alter this Privacy Policy.

Communications We will get in touch with you through email, calls, notices posted on our app, or through text messages or push notifications. The messages may be about the availability of our services, security or other service-related issues. So be aware that you cannot avoid receiving messages from us.

Contact Information If you have any queries or complaints regarding this Privacy Policy, then you can contact us on keralatribes@gmail.com
